I'm Charlot

A portuguese male cat with a moustache

Follow my adventures

The beggining

I was born in March 2019 in Arouca, a small village in the north of Portugal, in my parent’s family farm. My perfect black moustache caught their attention and they fell in love. They called me Charlot.

It’s good to be home

A few months later, I was adopted and my parents drove me to my forever home in Porto, Portugal. I was scared at first, but soon I realized that it was safe to start great adventures at home. My 3 cat sibblings and mother were adopted as well.

Friends around the world

My parents started recording it and created an Instagram account. Soon, I was noticed due to my moustache but got famous for climbing mirrors, chasing my tail or opening blinds and cabinets. I got featured on some of the most famous Instagram pages in the world, like Instagram, The Dodo, Cats of Instagram or Meowed.

Welcome Olívia

Along more than 30.000 friends around the world, in 2020, I welcomed Olívia as well. On a Sunday morning, my parents found and rescued her from the streets near our house. We were introduced carefully and soon we were sharing our adventures. We love to chase each other and sleep together during winter time.

Welcome Francisca

My parents decided to make the family grow and in 2021. My human sister Francisca was born. I love to sleep on top of her things. In her bed, in her clothes or in her dolls’ crib. Everything that has her smell. She loves to read me books and she is very respectful of my time and space.

Living my best life

Now, I’ve living with my family in my house. I couldn’t ask for more. Food whenever I want. Cuddles and kisses all the time (sometimes too much). Lots of mirrors to climb and cabinets to open. I couldn’t ask for more. Thank you fo being part of this big family.
